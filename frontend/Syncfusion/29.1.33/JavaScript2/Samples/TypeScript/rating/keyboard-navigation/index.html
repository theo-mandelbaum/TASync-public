Description

The below key combinations can be used in Rating to perform various actions. Tab - Focus. Left Arrow - Increase in RTL and decrease in LTR. Right Arrow - Decrease in RTL and increase in LTR. Down arrow - Decreases the value. Up arrow - Increases the value.
