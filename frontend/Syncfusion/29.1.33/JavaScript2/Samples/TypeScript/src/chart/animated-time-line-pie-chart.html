This sample demonstrates how the data source for the doughnut chart can dynamically update at regular intervals over a span of years.

In this example, you can see how to render and configure a doughnut chart that displays the average stock market prices of leading tech giants, with each entry featuring the company name and the corresponding stock price. The Doughnut chart updates dynamically to show the progression of stock prices from 2010 to 2020 using the setData method.
