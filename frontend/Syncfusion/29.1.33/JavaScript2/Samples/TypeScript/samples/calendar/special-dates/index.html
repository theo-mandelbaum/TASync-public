Special Dates sample demonstrates, how to customize a specific dates in a calendar by using renderDayCell event. This event gets triggered on each day cell element creation that allows you to customize or disable the specific dates in calendar. Here 10, 15 and 25 date's are customized with custom styles by adding the e-customStyle class. More information on the calendar customization can be found in this documentation section.
