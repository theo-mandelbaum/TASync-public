This sample demonstrates how the syncfusion Angular DataGrid, integrated with AI, can predict data based on the grid's existing entries

To explore this and more Syncfusion Angular Smart AI integrations locally, check out our GitHub repository.

In this example, the DataGrid displays information such as Student Name and ID, along with GPAs for the first, second, and third years. With AI, the Syncfusion Angular DataGrid can forecast the Final-year GPA, Total, and Grade. These predictions are shown in the grid when you click the "Calculate Grade" button
