This sample demonstrates how the syncfusion JavaScript DataGrid, powered by AI, can perform trend analysis on its data.

To explore this and more Syncfusion JavaScript Smart AI integrations locally, check out our GitHub repository.

In this demonstration, the DataGrid shows sales data, market spending, and details about new and returning customers over the past three years. When you click the "Trend Analysis" button, AI analyzes the existing trends and predicts future sales trends, highlighting the trend data in the DataGrid.
